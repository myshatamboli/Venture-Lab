# Venture Lab

### A collection of ideas, experiments, and opportunities worth exploring.

Venture Lab is where I document the projects that never make it into a pitch deck, the questions that don't have answers yet, and the ideas that might become something bigger.

Some concepts will fail.

Some will evolve.

A few may become ventures.

The goal isn't perfection, it's exploration.

---

## What Lives Here?

### Problem Discovery

Interesting problems, observations, and inefficiencies I've encountered in everyday life, education, healthcare, technology, and business.

### Venture Concepts

Early-stage startup ideas and opportunities that deserve further investigation.

### Market Research

Industries, trends, customer behaviours, and emerging technologies that capture my attention.

### Experiments

Tests, prototypes, frameworks, and assumptions I'm actively exploring.

### Lessons Learned

Insights from projects, competitions, leadership experiences, and entrepreneurial experiments.

## Why This Repository Exists

Most people only see finished products.

I'm interested in documenting the process.

The best ideas rarely appear fully formed—they emerge through curiosity, experimentation, research, and countless iterations.

This repository is a record of that process.
